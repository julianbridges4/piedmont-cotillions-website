Our Sedgefield Cotillion is held on Monday nights in October at the Sedgefield Country Club, Ross Course. We are looking forward to another wonderful year. We will continue to cover table manners, cell phone etiquette, and social networking etiquette. We are adding in the Salsa, Swing and a few new line dances as well – so be sure to get your dancing shoes ready. We offer a summer dinner/dance in June to review our table manners and enjoy a wonderful dinner.

Our Cary Cotillion is held on Tuesday nights in October at the MacGregor Downs Country Club. Please remember to hold your spot with your RSVP, as we are now starting a waiting list for this class, based on room capacity. We include more etiquette , covering not only table manners, but also cell phone and social networking etiquette. We also teach several ballrooms dances to include the Foxtrot and Tango! We look forward to seeing you soon.

Our Thomasville Cotillion is held at the Meadowlands Golf and Tennis Club. This fast growing cotillion is sure to fill up quickly this year, so be sure to register early and come out and be part of the fun. Learn how to Shag, Tango, and Foxtrot! Etiquette is the main part of our cotillion here, and is taught each night! These classes will run for five consecutive Tuesday nights throughout February. This group will also enjoy a summer dinner/dance for review and reconnecting.
